Honor and Prize

Dream Project Incubator - 2015

Dream Project Incubator is a program setup by MIT PhDs and PhD candidates along with Boston Global Forum. It aims to transform ambitious, self-driven young Vietnamese into better thinkers and doers via intellectual engagement and mentorship.

I was awarded a 3 months stay in Boston, and the chances to meet and confer with top minds from MIT and Havard University.

FAB12 Conference - 2016

Each year members of the more than 1,000 worldwide Fab Labs gather to share, collaborate, explore and, of course, fabricate.

In August 2016 FAB12 was organized in Shenzhen, China a historically important city for manufacturing and making. FabLab Vietnam Foundation (which I am a founding member) have been awarded with a $1,200 scholarship for 8 participant.
